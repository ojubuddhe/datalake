Who are we?

The Center for Data Science and Analytics (CDSA) was established in July 2023 with the overarching mission of fostering collaboration among researchers engaged in data-centric research endeavors at Ashoka University. CDSA is committed to serving as a comprehensive resource hub, offering robust support for data management and analysis across an array of academic departments and centers. These encompass the Department of Economics, the Department of Political Science, the Centre for Climate Change and Sustainability, the Trivedi School of Biosciences, the Koita Centre for Digital Health, the Centre for Interdisciplinary Archaeology Research, and numerous others. CDSA is poised to deliver integrated solutions for the storage, administration, and analysis of diverse datasets, while also facilitating cross-disciplinary data connections and amalgamations. Our forthcoming Data Lake initiative is set to commence operations shortly. Stay tuned for updates and developments in this space!
